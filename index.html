Location

Five minutes from home.
A world away from working alone.

Located at the intersection of Côte Saint‑Luc and Hampstead, BLINK offers the presence of a downtown office with the convenience of staying local.

A place people actually belong.

BLINK is designed for a specific community of nearby professionals who value quality, familiarity, and shared standards. This is not a transient coworking space. People recognize one another. Conversations continue. Proximity creates meaningful connection.

Experience

A space that feels good
to spend time in.

BLINK is designed from the inside out. Layout, lighting, materials, and flow are guided by how the space should feel throughout the day. Light that supports focus. Materials that add warmth. A flow that feels intuitive and calm. The result feels closer to a boutique hotel lounge than traditional coworking — composed, comfortable, and built for daily use.
